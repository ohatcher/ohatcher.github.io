Magic Music Box

Magic Music Box

2021
clay, paper mache, pompoms, arduino
6x4x4"

This interactive sculpture utilizes an arduino with a sensor to detect when the box is open or closed. Upon opening, a short tune is played while the figure spins in a circle. This project was influenced by a childhood jewelry box of mine, and it explores childlike wonder and the feeling of magic that occurs before learning how something works.
