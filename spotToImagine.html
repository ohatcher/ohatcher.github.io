A Spot to Imagine

A Spot To Imagine

2021
spray foam, clay, paint, pom-poms, gems
3x6'

This installation is about providing a space for people to sit and let their imaginations wander while looking at the sculptures and recording their thoughts and what they see in the notebook. The material choice and bright color palette have a very childlike quality to encourage viewers to engage with a sense of childlike wonder.
