About Me

Olivia Hatcher grew up in North Carolina and moved to Brooklyn in 2019 to study Interactive Arts at Pratt Institute. During this time, she developed her style and conceptual interests. Her work centers around creating and engaging with feelings of wonder, and encouraging imagination within viewers. While at Pratt, Olivia has exhibited work in the DDA gallery, and her senior year culminates in a group exhibition at One Art Space in Tribeca.
