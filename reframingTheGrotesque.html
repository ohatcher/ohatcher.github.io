Reframing the Grotesque

Reframing the Grotesque

2023
Interactive installation with LEDs, inductive power, sculpture, and projection

In this installation, I am focusing specifically on curiosity and letting my own sense of wonder guide the work. The subject is mold, framed as something to be in awe of rather than disgusted by. I spent a large amount of time conducting research by growing my own mold samples and observing them as they grew as well as with a microscope. I found it fascinating how the microscopic details of my petri dishes resembled some of the details one might find in a forest. Each mold sample was so unique and it was so exciting to explore all of the details and watch how they changed over time. I want people who interact with this work to feel that excitement and curiosity for themselves and to experience the joy of finding wonder in unexpected places.
