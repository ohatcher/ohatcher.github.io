Museum of Lost Toys

2021
found toys, arduino, touch capacitor, projection mapping
4x3'

This is an interactive project where participants can learn about the backstories of the different toys in the museum. The concept is about evoking memories of childhood within the participants, and to get them to see the toys in a more personified way. When each button is pressed, that toyâ€™s story and portrait is projected onto the table. The descriptions that show up when each button is pressed are formatted like a museum description card, and the content of the descriptions tell the story of how each toy ended up in the museum, similar to how a history museum might tell the background of a certain artifact.
